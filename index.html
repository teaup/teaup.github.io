Our vision

“Bubble tea, a bridge between west and east culture.“

Tea up is a company specializing in tea products. We want to bring authentic tea drinks to people around the world. We want to create an atmosphere where friends and family can spend quality time together to enjoy a cup of tasty bubble tea.

Come and discover!
